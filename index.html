Radiant Voices: Muslim
Women's Council in
Australia

The Muslim Women’s Council of Victoria Inc. (formerly known as the Islamic Girls & Women’s Group Inc.) a non-profit organization established in 1989. Its mission is to address the unique challenges encountered by Muslim women as they reside, work, and assimilate within the wider community while actively contributing to the well-being of the Australian community.
