Overview

Welcome to the Muslim Women's Council of Victoria!

Welcome to the Muslim Women’s Council of Victoria Inc. (MWCV), formerly known as the Islamic Girls & Women’s Group Inc. Established in 1989, we are a dedicated not-for-profit organization with a mission deeply rooted in supporting and empowering Muslim women as they navigate the challenges of living, working, and integrating into the broader Australian community.

Our journey began with the aspiration to address these challenges while preserving the rich tapestry of our members' religious and cultural heritage. We are proudly based in the City of Moreland, serving not only our local community but also extending our reach throughout Melbourne. With over 250 members, our commitment to fostering a strong sense of belonging and unity is unwavering.

Our Vision

Our vision is clear - we aim to make a positive impact on the Australian community. By addressing the unique challenges faced by marginalized communities, including new migrants, refugees, asylum seekers, and international students, we strive to foster a more inclusive and supportive society.

Acknowledgement of Country

As an organization deeply rooted in the values of respect and acknowledgment, we pay our respects to the Traditional Owners of the land throughout Australia. We recognize their enduring connection to the land, waters, and culture, and we extend our gratitude to their Elders, both past and present.

Mission

Our mission is clear and unwavering: to help meet the challenges faced by marginalized communities. Whether supporting new migrants, refugees, asylum seekers, or international students, we are dedicated to creating pathways for empowerment, resilience, and integration.

Join us in our journey to build a more compassionate and supportive Australian community. Together, we can make a lasting difference.

Objectives

Our organization is driven by a set of clear objectives aimed at improving the lives of individuals facing continual or temporary challenges. These objectives include:

  • Addressing Key Challenges: We aim to help individuals grappling with challenges such as unemployment, housing affordability, and food shortages by providing support and resources.
  • Holistic Support for Marginalized Communities: Our focus extends beyond Muslim women to include members of all marginalized communities, including new migrants, refugees, asylum seekers, and international students. Through mental and physical health initiatives, we strive to enhance the well-being of these communities.
  • Promoting Understanding of Islam: We are dedicated to improving the general community's understanding of Islam, fostering an environment of knowledge, respect, and inclusivity.
  • Cultural Exchange: By providing non-Muslims with the opportunity to learn about the Muslim culture and actively participate in Muslim traditions, we seek to bridge gaps and build stronger connections between diverse communities.
  • Harmony and Integration: Our commitment to improving harmony between people from different backgrounds and religions underscores our belief in a cohesive and integrated society.
