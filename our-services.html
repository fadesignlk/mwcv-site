Our Services

Championing Change: Our Commitment to Women's Progress

Our purpose is to help improve the lives of not just Muslim women but members of marginalised communities including: new migrants, refugees, asylum seekers and international students. We do this through the provision of emergency food relief, education and social support.

We are physically located in Brunswick, however our doors are open to anyone seeking support, irrespective of where they live. We receive referrals from organisations as well as self-referrals from individuals.

Catering

Our vegetarian/halal catering service provides migrants and refugees with paid employment. The food tastes and smells as delicious as it looks. We cater for small and large events and will work with you to meet your needs.

Settlement Services

A large focus of our work in 2022 and 2023 has been providing settlement services to Afghani refugees to help them settle and integrate into the community. This includes delivering English classes, Sewing classes, running sessions on Australian culture, housing support and referrals to other support services.

Read More
Emergency Food Relief

Our emergency food relief program distributes food parcels to the homeless and people struggling to put food on the table. An average week sees us distribute 70-100 food parcels. This program is supported by donations from Second Bite, Foodbank and members of the community.

One Coffee, One Chat

These bi-monthly events facilitate community conversations and improve the wider community's understanding of Islam. We create a welcoming atmosphere to share our culture and traditions with participants and encourage healthy debate and discussion on difficult topics.

Sport and Recreation

We are currently conducting research on the availability of women-only sport and recreation opportunities around Melbourne.
